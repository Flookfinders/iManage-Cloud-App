You will find the Search box at the top right of the page e.g.

The Search function allows you to search for a Street or Property address as well as a USRN, UPRN or a Cross Reference.

•If you are searching for an address then, as you start typing, matching entries are displayed from which you may select the address you require e.g.

Selecting an entry from the list of results will display the Street or Property form depending on what type of result you clicked on. Note: If you were on the home page when you performed your search you will be taken to the Gazetteer page.
If you just press enter rather than selecting an address from the results the list of matching entries is displayed on the Gazetteer page down the left-hand side in an Explorer view.

•To search for a USRN, UPRN or Cross Reference enter the details and press enter. The search will check first for a USRN, then a UPRN and then a Cross Reference
