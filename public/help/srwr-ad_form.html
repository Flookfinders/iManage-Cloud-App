(Scotland only)

All the details associated with the Associated Data (AD) for a street are displayed when you click on the ASD tab of the Street form. Note: You can click Expand all to show all of the records or just click on a type to expand that particular type of Associated Data.

Maintenance Responsibility details

(Scotland only)

Maintenance Responsibility (Type 51) data provides information relating to the authority or authorities responsible for maintaining a road.

Notes:

•There should be at least one Maintenance Responsibility record for every Type 1 or Type 2 street.

•More than one record may exist for a road of Types 1 or 2 only if sections of the road are maintained by more than one responsible authority e.g. roads authority and frontages.

•Maintenance Responsibility records are only allowed on a Type 1 or Type 2 street.

Maintenance Responsibility details are displayed on the ASD tab of the Street form.

Record

Sequential number for each Maintenance responsibility record for a street displayed at the top of the form e.g. (1 of 1).

Street Status

The status of the road.

Custodian

The organisation providing the data.

Authority

The organisation maintaining the street.

Start Date

This is the date the responsibility started.

End Date

The date on which this organisation ceased maintaining the street.

State

Current state of the maintenance responsibility record. This can be either Current or Historic.

Applied to

Indicates whether the information applies to the Whole road or Part of road. If Part of road is selected then the following extra information is required.

Specify location

If you have specified Part of road then you need to enter a description of the location of the part or parts of the street for which this Maintenance Responsibility is applicable.

ASD geometry

If you have specified Part of road then you need to draw the geometry for the location of the part or parts of the street for which this Maintenance Responsibility is applicable, using the drawing tools.
Reinstatement Category details

(Scotland only)

Reinstatement Categories (Type 52) data provides information relating to the reinstatement category of a road or of particular sections or elements of a road.

Notes:

•There should be at least one Reinstatement Category record for every Type 1 or Type 2 street.

•More than one record may exist for a road of Types 1 or 2 only if sections of the road are of different reinstatement categories. e.g. a major traffic route turning off a particular road leaving the remainder with limited traffic or the road having foot-ways which are, of course, of different reinstatement category from the carriageway.

•Reinstatement Category records are only allowed on a Type 1 or Type 2 street.

Reinstatement Category details are displayed on the ASD tab of the Street form.

Record

Sequential number for each Reinstatement category record for a street displayed at the top of the form e.g. (1 of 1).

Reinstatement category

The reinstatement type e.g. Carriageway Type 1 - 10 to 30 MSA.

Custodian

The organisation providing the data.

Authority

The Associated Road Works Register Data authority specifying the reinstatement category.

Start Date

The date on which the reinstatement category took effect in the real world.

End Date

Date on which this reinstatement category ceased to exist in the real world.

State

Current state of the reinstatement category record. This can be either Current or Historic.

Applied to

Indicates whether the information applies to the Whole road or Part of road. If Part of road is selected then the following extra information is required.

Specify location

If you have specified Part of road then you need to enter a description of the location of the part or parts of the street for which this Reinstatement Category is applicable.

ASD geometry

If you have specified Part of road then you need to draw the geometry for the location of the part or parts of the street for which this Reinstatement Category is applicable, using the drawing tools.
Special Designation details

(Scotland only)

Special Designations (Type 53) data provides information relating to special designations applying to the road in general; to sections of the road or to specific points on the road.

Notes:

•Special Designation records are only allowed on a Type 1 or Type 2 street.

•A road may have no special designations applying to it but equally may have several.

Special Designation details are displayed on the ASD tab of the Street form.

Record

Sequential number for each Special designation record for a street displayed at the top of the form e.g. (1 of 1).

Type

The type of special designation to which the record applies e.g. Traffic Sensitive, Protect Road etc.

Custodian

The organisation responsible for providing the data.

Authority

The Associated Road Works Register Data authority responsible for the special designation.

Description

Description providing additional information.

Start Date

The date on which the special designation started to apply in the real world.

End Date

Date on which this special designation ceased to apply in the real world.

State

Current state of the special designation record. This can be either Current or Historic.

Applied to

Indicates whether the information applies to the Whole road or Part of road. If Part of road is selected then the following extra information is required.

Specify location

If you have specified Part of road then you need to enter a description of the location of the part or parts of the street for which this Special Designation is applicable.

ASD geometry

If you have specified Part of road then you need to draw the geometry for the location of the part or parts of the street for which this Special Designation is applicable, using the drawing tools.
