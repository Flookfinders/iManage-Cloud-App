Once you have performed a search and pressed enter or selected a street from the results, the results are displayed in the Explorer.

The icons displayed to the left of the text are different depending on whether the result is a Street or a Property and, if it is a Property whether it is a Parent or Street record, or if the classification is Commercial or Residential. The icons are also coloured differently depending upon the State of the Street or Logical Status of the Property. For Streets the icon includes a number denoting the Street Type.

Note: The Explorer is a straight list of results and does not show details such as parent / child relationships. If you wish to see this information you need to first pick the street from the results and then click the Related tab.
