Highway Dedication details

 Top  Previous  Next

(England & Wales only)

The Highway Dedication (Type 17) holds a description of who is allowed to use the street, for this section of ESU, together with seasonal information and whether or not the ESU is subject to certain restrictions such as being a Public Right of Way. This information is displayed when you click on the ESU tab of the Street form.

To display the Highway Dedications for the ESU either click on the > beside the ESU entry, or click on the ESU in the list and select the entry from the list of Highway dedications under the ESU information.

Type

This represents the type of highway dedication that applies to this section of the Street.

Dedication start

This is the date that the Highway Dedication legally started. If the Highway Dedication has a specified time period when the designation starts then the Time may be specified.

Dedication end

This is the date the Highway Dedication legally ended. If the Highway Dedication has a specified time period when the designation ends then the Time may be specified.

Indicators

If any of the boxes are ticked then this means that the information applies to this section of the Street.

Description

Seasonal start

If the Highway Dedication is seasonal or periodical, this is the date when the Highway Dedication starts.

Seasonal end

If the Highway Dedication is seasonal or periodical, this is the date when the Highway Dedication ends.

Record end

The date that the Highway Dedication record ended.
