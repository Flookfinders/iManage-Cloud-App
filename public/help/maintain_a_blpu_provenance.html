Maintaining Provenances

 Top  Previous  Next

First you need to find the property that you wish to maintain. You can use the Search to search for the property by UPRN or address. Either select the property from the search results or press enter in the search and select the property from the Explorer. The details for the property are displayed in the Property form.

Note: The provenance extents (polygons) are not visible on the map by default but you can make them visible by selecting the map Layer Control and turning the visibility on for the Background provenance layer.

•Click on the Provenances tab.

Add a Provenance

•Select to Add new Provenance from one of the Provenance Actions.

•Change the Provenance details as required.

•If you wish to create a polygon for your Provenance use the Draw a polygon tool from the Map toolbar, then click on the map for each node of your polygon and double click when you have finished. You may then move or delete nodes as required. For further details please see Maintaining Provenance extents.

•Once you are happy, click OK to validate your entry.

•Now click Save to update the property.

Editing a Provenance

•Select the Provenance that you wish to change.

•Change the Provenance details as required.

•If you wish to change or add a polygon please see Maintaining Provenance extents.

•When you are happy, click OK to validate your changes.

•Now click Save to update the property.

Deleting a Provenance

Delete a single Provenance

•Select the Provenance that you wish to delete.

•Select to Delete Provenance from one of the Provenance Actions.

•You will see a confirmation dialog letting you know what will be deleted if you continue e.g.

•Click to delete the Provenance or click CANCEL to return without deleting.

•Now click Save to update the property.

Note: If you wish to just delete the Provenance polygon, then use the select tool to select the polygon on the map and then click Delete feature from the Map tools. This will leave the Provenance record in the database and just delete the polygon.

Delete multiple Provenances

•Select the Provenances you wish to delete from the list.

•A dialog is displayed showing the number of records you have selected with a delete icon e.g.

•Click the Delete Provenance records icon.

•You will see a confirmation dialog letting you know what will be deleted if you continue e.g.

•Click to delete the Provenance or click CANCEL to return without deleting.

•Now click Save to update the property.
