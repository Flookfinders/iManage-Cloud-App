District Lookups

District Lookups are only available for English and Welsh Gazetteer with the ASD module. These may be maintained by clicking on the Lookup tables under Customisation in the Settings and selecting Districts from the list.

A list of Districts is displayed. Note: The only entries that you will be allowed to maintain are the entries for your own authority as defined on the Authority details in the Settings. You can use the Import function of the iManage Utilities application to import an ODD file for other authorities.

There are five boxes displayed for the different areas of the district details. Click on a link for the details for each area.

•To delete a District, hover over it in the list and click on the Delete icon. You now have the option of deleting the entry or making it historic e.g.

•If you select to Make Historic it will set the Closed Date for the District and set the Status to Historic.

•If you select Delete forever all of the details will be deleted.
