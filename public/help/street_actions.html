Explorer Street actions

 Top  Previous  Next

Hovering over a street or selecting it in the Explorer displays a toolbar with various actions and selecting More actions or More respectively will display further actions e.g.

If you hover over a street

If you select a single street

 

Action

Description

Add property

Add a property to the street

Add properties

Add a range of properties to the street

Copy USRN

Copy the USRN to the clipboard

Open in Street View

Display the street in Street View. The Street Start coordinates are passed to Google and Google displays the nearest street to those coordinates

Zoom to this

Zoom to the street on the map

Remove from list

Allows you to remove the street from the Explorer list.

Close street

Close the street

Delete

Delete the street
