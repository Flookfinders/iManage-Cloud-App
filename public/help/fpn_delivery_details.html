FPN (Fixed Penalty Notice) Delivery details may be changed by an Administrator from the Settings > Lookup tables > Districts and selecting the district you wish to maintain.

Delivery URL

This is the web service URL for receiving Fixed Penalty Notices.

Address

Postal address for the delivery of Fixed Penalty Notices.

Postcode

Post code for the delivery of Fixed Penalty Notices.

Email address

Email Address for the delivery of Fixed Penalty Notices.

Delivery Fax No

Fax number for the delivery of Fixed Penalty Notices.

 

Make the changes that you require and then click Done.
