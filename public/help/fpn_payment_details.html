FPN Payment details

 Top  Previous  Next

FPN (Fixed Penalty Notice) Payment details may be changed by an Administrator from the Settings > Lookup tables > Districts and selecting the district you wish to maintain.

Payment URL

URL for payment of Fixed Penalty Notices by credit card via the authority's website.

Bank name

The bank name for payment of Fixed Penalty Notices by BACS.

Account name

The account name for payment of Fixed Penalty Notices by cheque (by post).

Account no.

Account number for payment of Fixed Penalty Notices by BACS.

Sort code

The sort code for payment of Fixed Penalty Notices by BACS.

Address

The postal address for payment of Fixed Penalty Notices by cheque (post or in person).

Postcode

The post code for payment of Fixed Penalty Notices by cheque (post or in person).

Telephone no.

The contact telephone number for payment by credit or debit card.

 

Make the changes that you require and then click Done.
