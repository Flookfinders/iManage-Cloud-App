FPN Contact details

FPN (Fixed Penalty Notice) Contact details may be changed by an Administrator from the Settings > Lookup tables > Districts and selecting the district you wish to maintain.

Contact name

The name of the officer to whom representations should be made.

Address

Postal address for representations in writing.

Postcode

Post code for representations in writing.

Telephone no.

Telephone number for the officer to whom representations should be made.

Permit scheme ID

The national permit scheme reference.

Out of hours arrangements made

Applies to street authorities only and indicates if the authority can receive and respond to notifications during non-working hours.

 

Make the changes that you require and then click Done.
