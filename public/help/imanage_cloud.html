When you first log on to iManage Cloud you will be taken to the Home page.

Click on either of the Home or Gazetteer icons to take you to the relevant page in iManage Cloud or use the Search to find an address.

 

Help

Clicking the Help icon at the top right of the page displays the Help and Support information. This is also where the application Metadata information may be found. If you scroll down, at the bottom there is a Copy metadata information link so that you can copy this information into any Case you may raise with the Idox Customer Portal as this can be useful in diagnosing any issues.

Log out

At the bottom left of the page you will see an icon with your initial e.g.

This is where you can Log out of iManage Cloud.
