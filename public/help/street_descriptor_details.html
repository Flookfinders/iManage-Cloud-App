Street Descriptor details

 Top  Previous  Next

Details associated with the Street Descriptor (Type 15) are displayed on the Street tab of the Street form.

Click on the entry to display the language dependent information for the street. This will normally be just an English entry (ENG) but with bilingual Gazetteers, you may view the other details by selecting the other language record e.g. CYM. If you hover over the Street Descriptor you can select to Copy address to clipboard which will copy the name of the street to the clipboard.

Language

The language identifying the language in use for the descriptive identifier. This will always be ENG unless your authority is a dual language authority.

Name or descriptor

This is the name or description of the street.

Locality

This is the neighbourhood, suburb, district, village or parish that may form part of a town or which may exist in its own right within the context of an administrative area, where the currently selected street is located. (This could apply to an industrial estate containing streets)

Town

This is the name of the town in which the currently selected street is located.

Island

(Scotland only)

The name of the Island where the street is located.

Admin Area

This is the name of the Administrative area in which the street resides.
